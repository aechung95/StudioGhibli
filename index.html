About

As an animation and Ghibli enthusiast, this page is dedicated to Studio Ghibli, a Japanese animation film studio. Ghibli films are renowned for their stunning art and deep themes that have been impressionable for adult and children audiences alike.
This is a personal appreciation site, with no affiliation to the actual studio. All opinions are my own.
